01

Overview

Prodaigi is a youth football team management platform operated by Somnira ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use the Prodaigi iOS application ("the App").

Prodaigi is intended for adult users only — coaches and parents. Players (minors) do not create accounts, log in, or otherwise interact with the App directly. Player profile data is entered by adult coaches for internal team management purposes only.

02

Who Uses Prodaigi

The App has two types of adult users:

  • Coaches (Head Coaches and Coaches): Manage team rosters, create practice plans, draw plays, schedule events, and communicate with parents.
  • Parents / Guardians: View team schedules, receive AI-generated training updates about their linked child, and communicate with coaches.

Children under 13 do not have accounts and do not use the App. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from any child under 13.

04

How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above solely to:

  • Authenticate your account and maintain your session
  • Display team rosters, schedules, plays, and messages within your team
  • Deliver push notifications from your team
  • Generate AI-powered practice plans and coaching summaries (for subscribed users) using OpenAI's API — only aggregated team context and first-name references are sent; no full personally identifiable information is included in AI prompts
  • Process subscription payments via Apple's App Store (we do not see or store payment card information)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

05

Children's Privacy — COPPA Compliance

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) applies to the online collection of personal information from children under 13.

COPPA Statement
Prodaigi does not collect personal information directly from children under 13. The App is designed for and marketed exclusively to adult coaches and parents. Children are not users of the App.

Player profile data (such as a player's name, position, and coach notes) is entered by adult coaches within a closed team environment. This data is:

  • Accessible only to members of the specific team (coaches and linked parents)
  • Never used for advertising or shared with third parties
  • Deletable at any time by the head coach or by contacting us directly

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at greggrodgers.ii@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Port your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email greggrodgers.ii@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Request." We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your account in-app: Profile → Settings → Delete Account. This removes all personal data associated with your account. Player profiles you created will be anonymized or deleted from the team roster.

09

Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext
  • All API communication uses HTTPS / TLS
  • Authentication uses short-lived JWTs (15 minutes) with rotating refresh tokens
  • Our server infrastructure is hosted on Hostinger with restricted access controls

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to greggrodgers.ii@gmail.com.
